Most formal council task contracts and session-review packages are omitted from the public repo.

In the full working system, this area preserves scoped council deliveries and task-level decision records.

Together with `user/data/sessions/`, it forms part of the future training substrate for a stronger middle layer.

The public repo keeps only selected, redacted case notes where the task shape demonstrates a reusable HELM pattern:

- `platform_capture_review/` — staged runtime repair and reviewer-gated verification
- `operations-stewardship-rollout/` — UserOps memory, task-state, closure, and Council re-entry discipline
- `local-role-pack-experiment/` — early local/API role-pack practice for Council and Executor identities

The public `userops/` folder contains the reusable form of the operations-steward layer.
